Pomegranates!

Author: Devin

Date Posted: 2023-08-02

Ahh, the pomegranate. Red on the outside, red on the inside, with red seeds. The only non-red part would be the white barriers in the center dividing the seed pods. Truly the reddest of fruits.

The pomegranate is said to have many red seeds inside of it, all with juice as red as the pomegranate's skin. What isn't there to love about this crimson berry?
