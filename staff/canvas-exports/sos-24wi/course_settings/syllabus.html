A tip from Academic Technologies:

Make the Assignments tab visible to students in addition to, or instead of, the Syllabus tab.

Including the "Assignments" tab in the Course Navigation Menu allows students two options for assignment list views via the "Show By" buttons available in the student's perspective of Canvas:

Date

  • Assignments are listed by date and grouped by what is Overdue, Undated, Upcoming, and Past (with Overdue showing at top, and Past at bottom)

Type

  • Assignments are grouped by Assignment Groups determined and named by Faculty (e.g. Seminar Papers, Essays, Film Reviews, etc)
